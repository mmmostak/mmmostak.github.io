my Volunteer Experience

General Member

Volunteer for Bangladesh (VBD)

Volunteer for Bangladesh is a nation-wide association of Volunteer Action Groups that work for the betterment of their individual communities and the nation as a whole. Volunteer Action Groups are registered and approved volunteer bodies, who will work in a certain area or community. These Groups must maintain certain outlined criteria in respect to performance and activity. All of the Volunteer Action Groups of one community or area will be monitored by a local board, which will in turn be monitored by one single nation board. The boards are responsible for making sure that the Volunteer Action Groups perform adequately and are attentive to their duties.
